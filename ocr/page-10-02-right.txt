T-systems with structure §$(V), then 0(0)

is the category of fuzzy systems. It has

been argued [1], [4], that V-sets represent
inexact concepts and are the building
blocks of inexact structures; it has also
been argued [11] that fuzzy systems of
various sorts (including fuzzy algorithms

{12]) are important for understanding such

pervasive phenomena as optimization in an

inexact environment,

Our notion of fuzzy system, including
the hierarchies of system structure and
arbitrary T and I is much more general
than anything we know in the literature.
And we hope more applicable, because it is
more structured. For example, fuzzy systems
can probably model word association nets
of the sort metaphorically described by
