If X and Y are sets, let PF[X,Y] denote
the set of all partial functions from X to
Y, that is, functions to Y from a subset
of X, and let df denote the domain of
f € PF[X,Y]. Defining composition the
obvious way with inverse image, there
results a category of partial morphisms of
sets. This construction can be duplicated
in many categories.

idealization in the construction of the
