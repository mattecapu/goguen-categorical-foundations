To model one system by or in another, we
want morphisms of diagrams, and a category
of diagrams (or systems) in 0.

Definition

A morphism from S:D + 0 to S':D' +O isa
pair <F,n>, where F:D + D' is a functor
and n:S'F + S is a natural transformation,
See Fig.5.

Denote by D(0) the category of diagrams
in O with these morphisms. This category
is similar to the category of fuzzy sets
with values in 0 [3], except the trans-
formation n goes the opposite way.
