Definition
A T-system is a diagram in O7, that is, a
functor S:D + Or, where D is called the
index category or diagram scheme.

The intended interpretation is: when two
morphisms meet at an object, they shall
display within that object identical
representations of the objects at their
domains. The conventions and consequences
of this interpretation can be developed
without category theory; we hope the
translation in Fig.4 of the graphs in
Fig.3 suffice.

The formalisation of the behavior of
systems in the next section makes it
explicit within our categorical framework.
