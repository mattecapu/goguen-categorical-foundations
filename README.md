## A digitisation of J. Goguen's _Categorical foundations for general systems theory_

Thanks to an anonymous helper, I obtained a scan of J. Goguen's _Categorical foundations for general systems theory_.
In the interest of preserving it for the mathematical community and making it publicly available, I semi-manually digitised it using Tesseract and the help of Claude.
You can read it [here](https://github.com/mattecapu/goguen-categorical-foundations/blob/master/goguen-categorical-foundations.pdf).
The Tesseract output, as well as the source LaTeX for the PDF, are available in this repo.

I hope other readers will find the paper as enticing and ahead of its time as I did.
