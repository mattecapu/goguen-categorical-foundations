## A digitisation of J. Goguen's _Categorical foundations for general systems theory_

Thanks to an anonymous helper, I obtained a scan of J. Goguen's _Categorical foundations for general systems theory_, which is otherwise unavailable.
In the interest of preserving it for the mathematical community and making it available, I semi-manually digitised it using Tesseract and the help of Claude.

I hope other readers will find the paper as enticing and ahead of its time as I did.
